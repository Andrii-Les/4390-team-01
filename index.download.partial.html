Use Our App

Download the UDRIVE app to your smartphone and book a car in a couple of seconds. You can find the closest cars to your location, book, unlock, and lock them right in the app.

It's Safe

We do not require almost any personal information (except for the driver's license) from you and do not store your data.

It's Fast

You can book a car in just a couple of clicks. All you need is to open the city map and select the vehicle.

You manage the car directly in the application and make payment there when you park the rented car in the selected place. Everything is very simple, fast, and money-saving!
